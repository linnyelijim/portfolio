About Us

Lead Me Outdoors is a service that allows travelers to search for hikes within their desired National Park. Once on the park page, you will have the option to take a short quiz that will provide you with personalized trail suggestions.

We created this feature in hopes of encouraging people of all ages, levels, and physical conditions to get outside and truly enjoy nature. We hope to take the unknown out of choosing a hike and make sure you end up on a trail that fits you.
